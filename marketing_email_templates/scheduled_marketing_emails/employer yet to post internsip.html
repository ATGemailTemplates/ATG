Across The Globe (ATG) is a platform to where you can post internships and jobs for free and you can promote your business organisation.

Why Across The Globe (ATG) ?

  • You can post internships for free.
  • From posting internships, managing applicants, hiring candidate. You can do everything smoothly in one dashboard.
  • Reviewing applications, communication and hiring process everything can be done online.
  • Recruitment software automatically sends notifications and reminders to the candidates at every stage of hiring process.
  • You can chat with applicants and create hiring groups. Now you don't need any third party platform for that.
