Across The Globe (ATG) is platform where employers/recruiters can hire employees/interns and promote their business organisation.

 

Any business organisation can create a business profile on Across the Globe (ATG). You can post jobs and internships and find suitable candidates. You can message and chat with the applicants. You can manage and track the applicants. You can also carry out the entire hiring process on Across The Globe (ATG) itself.

We recommend you to include following in your job post.

  • Title should be clearly descriptive of the job.
  • Use industry-specific job titles, these are the keywords candidates will use in a job search.
  • Don't forget to mention the required skills and salary.
  • Company's objective and work location should be mentioned.
  • Employee's day-to-day key responsibilities should be mentioned.
