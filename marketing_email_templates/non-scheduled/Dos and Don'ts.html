The Dos

  • Be Specific and clearly state required qualifications and skills.
  • Use relevant job titles Stick to industry-specific job titles these
    are the keywords candidates will use in a job search.
  • Use simple language to make things easier to understand.

The Don'ts

  • Overload credentials Posting stacked with preferred credentials
    is enough to scare away even the most qualified candidates.
  • Avoid salary Candidates often complain about job postings that
    do not provide information on compensation.
  • Forget the spell check Double check for spelling, grammar,
    and inconsistencies, as mistakes can make your company
    look bad.
